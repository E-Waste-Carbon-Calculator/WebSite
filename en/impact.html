Did you know?

The European Union (EU) has adopted the 2020 Circular Economy Action Plan (CEAP), creating a policy framework that normalizes sustainable products, services, and businesses. This plan includes regulations like the Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR), the Directive on the Repair of Goods, and the Green Claims Directive, which establish requirements for sustainable product performance and information.

Under the Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR), new ecodesign requirements will apply to mobile phones and tablets sold in the EU market starting on 20 June 2025. These requirements will include detailed specifications on durability, repairability, reusability, and recyclability, as well as access to product information.
