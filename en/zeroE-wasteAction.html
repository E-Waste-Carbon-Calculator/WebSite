✓Adopt green product design based on 3 principles

  • Design:

    Consider the environmental attributes of the product—such as disassembly, recyclability, maintainability, and reusability—which in turn simplify the recycling processes for decommissioned electronic products.

  • Materials used:

    Employ eco-friendly new materials that are non-hazardous, energy-saving, easily recyclable, highly reliable, and have a long lifespan.

  • Contribution techniques:

    Develop green recycling processes for electronic products to help avoid the release of harmful substances during the dismantling of e- waste, and to reduce the use of acidic, alkaline, and corrosive reagents and chemicals.
