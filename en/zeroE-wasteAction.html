Adopt green product design based on 3 principles

  • Design:

    Consider the environmental attributes of the product, such as disassembly, recyclability, maintainability, and reusability. These factors can simplify the recycling processes for decommissioned electronic products.

  • Materials used:

    Utilize eco-friendly materials that are non-hazardous, energy-efficient, easily recyclable, highly reliable, and have a long lifespan.

  • Contribution techniques:

    Develop sustainable recycling processes for electronic products to prevent the release of harmful substances during e-waste dismantling. Aim to minimize the use of acidic, alkaline, and corrosive reagents and chemicals.
